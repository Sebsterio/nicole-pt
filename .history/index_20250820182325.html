Hi, I'm Nicole

- your dedicated fitness coach specialising in supporting women through life’s transformative moments: pregnancy, postpartum, and menopause.

I’m a certified Personal Trainer based in West London with a unique background. I began my professional journey as a fashion model, which sparked my passion for fitness and health. Later, I transitioned into personal training, driven by a desire to help others achieve their best selves. After experiencing debilitating health issues, I committed myself to furthering my education and understanding of holistic health. My goal is to help women lead healthier, more joyful lives by reconnecting with their bodies, reclaiming their strength, and rediscovering their full potential. I strongly believe that results on the outside begin on the inside first. That’s why I focus not only physical appearance but more importantly improving the wellbeing and inner strength. My approach is about empowering you to feel strong, confident, and vibrant in your everyday life.

Programmes What I can help you with

Programme General

Fitness

Perfect for women who want to become fitter, stronger, and more toned rather than losing a large amount of weight. The training plan is based on strength, functional training, pilates and mobility work.

Programme Posture

Correction

Improve your health, body comfort and posture through a combination of strength, functional training, pilates, mobility drills, and fascia release.

Are you an office worker? Do you have a sedentary lifestyle? Do you have back discomfort after sitting for a few hours? Then this is the programme for you.
Disclaimer: This is not a programme for people who are injured. This has to be addressed by a medical doctor.

Programme Body

Transformation

Body transformation is for women who want to lose 5+kg, become stronger, more confident and have visible muscle definition. Beside training, it includes nutrition, accountability, and mindset coaching as this is what is required to make a real change that will last long term.

We will remodel your physique and transform your mindset. You will learn how to program your training and nutrition to achieve long-term results. It will require dedication and commitment, but far from being unsustainable. No fad diets, or daily workouts.

Programme Pre & Post-Natal

The goal is to address all of your fitness needs throughout your pregnancy. We will focus on strengthening muscles that become weak and releasing muscles that become tight due to changes that happen to your body during pregnancy.

For new mums it’s important to return to exercise with a strategy to help your body heal and recover safely.
We will strengthen the deep core, the pelvic floor, the back and glute muscles, as well as practice diaphragmatic breathing and release tight areas. Postpartum recovery helps to tighten muscles from the inside out, which is especially important if we want to improve the appearance of our stomach.

Programme Menopause

A dedicated programme for women who are going through menopause. We will address muscle mass preservation, bone health, joint integrity, and cardiovascular health. The focus will be on strength, functional training, core work and improving cardiovascular training.

Cardio for menopausal women isn't about "burning calories" — it's a tool for heart health, blood sugar control, mood regulation, and overall resilience. It complements strength training rather than replacing it.

My Story Fitness is Life

For nearly a decade, I’ve been passionate about researching and promoting evidence-based, sustainable techniques that empower women to feel strong, confident, and healthy at every stage of life. Sport has been my passion from an early age; having grown up as a competing athlete, being active was an integral part of my upbringing.

After college, I ventured into the world of fashion modelling, where I discovered my love for gym workouts and running. I experimented with various fitness and nutrition programs to achieve that "model physique." This eight-year journey of personal experimentation and study provided me with invaluable insights into maintaining optimal health and fitness.

While obtaining my personal training certification opened doors to working with clients, I quickly realised that true health and wellness require more than just exercise. It’s about understanding each individual's unique goals, lifestyle, posture, and fitness level. My own experiences with injuries motivated me to deepen my knowledge so I can help others avoid similar setbacks.

In today’s world, where many of us spend most of our days sitting—whether at work, at home, or commuting—simply hitting the gym isn’t enough. We need to look at the bigger picture: how the body moves, mobility and stability limitations, muscle tightness, and overall body function.

That’s why I provide a holistic approach that integrates strength training, mobility, stability, and fascia release.
